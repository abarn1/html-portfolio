Top 3 Movies

Your Name (君の名は)

Absolutely gorgeous animation combined with a really fascinating story with a fun little twist thrown in.

The Secret Life of Walter Mitty

Not the most amazing movie but I really like the story and the cinematography and locations are pretty fun to watch.

Fight Club

I like the message of not just doing what someone tells you to do and there are the fun little easter eggs to go back and watch for in the movie.
